QuikShut

A simple, smart tool to control when your PC powers off.

ABOUT

QuikShut is a simple, efficient method of choosing when you want your PC to shut down. It was designed with maintainence and downloading operations in mind, allowing you to turn off your PC after it's operations are complete.

DOWNLOAD

QuikShut will always be completely free. However, if you'd like to contribute in order to sustain this project and general development. You can choose to do so of your own accord. It will be greatly appreciated!
